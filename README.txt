Character traits:
1. Heavy armor 
	-Has slow acceleration
	-However, has high speed (takes time to reach full speed)
	-Initial speed is slow

2. Can only wield stubby swords
	-Stab 
	-> High penetration, High damage, Single target
	
	-Slash 
	-> Low penetration, Low damage, Wide area
	
	-Throw 
	-> Speed drop off (initial high, then slows)
	-> Short range, Low penetration, Low damage, Single target.
	

3. Avoidability
	-Has a dash (no i-frame), distance determined by armor
	-Deflect damage probability (armor based)
	

Enemy traits:
1. Regular mobs
	-Damage by collision
	-Low armor, High HP
	-Continuos track player
	
2. Armored mobs
	-Damage by projectile
	-High armor, Low HP
	-LOS tracking
